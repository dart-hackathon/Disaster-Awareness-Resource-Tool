First Aid Guide

Your comprehensive resource for emergency medical response. Learn life-saving techniques and be prepared for any situation.

Emergency? Call Now: Dial 112 for immediate medical assistance across India

CPR

  • Check the scene is safe and call for emergency help
  • Check for responsiveness and breathing
  • Start chest compressions (100-120 per minute)
  • Give rescue breaths after 30 compressions
  • Continue CPR until help arrives

Burns

  • Remove from heat source immediately
  • Cool under running water (10+ minutes)
  • Remove jewelry and tight clothing
  • Cover with sterile bandage
  • Seek medical attention if severe

Choking

  • Encourage coughing if conscious
  • Give 5 back blows between shoulder blades
  • Perform abdominal thrusts if needed
  • Alternate between back blows and thrusts
  • Call emergency services if obstruction persists

Fractures

  • Keep the injured area still
  • Apply ice to reduce swelling
  • Check circulation beyond the injury
  • Immobilize the injured area
  • Seek immediate medical attention

Bleeding

  • Apply direct pressure to wound
  • Elevate the injured area
  • Apply clean bandage firmly
  • Add more bandages if needed
  • Seek medical care for severe bleeding

Heat Stroke

  • Move to a cool place
  • Remove excess clothing
  • Apply cool, wet cloths
  • Fan continuously
  • Give water if fully conscious
